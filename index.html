About WasteWise

WasteWise is an AI-powered waste classification tool built to help people sort waste correctly, reduce landfill contamination, and understand their environmental impact.

Our vision model is trained on 50,000+ labelled waste images and powered by Claude AI for natural language explanations. Local disposal rules are sourced from municipal databases across 340+ Indian cities.

Built with ♻️ in India · Version 3.0 · 2026

Privacy Policy

📷 Images

Photos you upload are sent to our AI model for classification and are not stored on our servers. They are deleted immediately after processing.

📊 Usage data

Scan history is stored only in your browser's local session and never uploaded to our servers.

📍 Location

Location is used only to look up local disposal rules. We do not track your GPS or store location history.

How it works

Three steps to smarter recycling

1
Snap or upload a photo
Take a picture of any waste item — packaging, food scraps, electronics, clothing. Any angle, any lighting works.
2
AI classifies in 1–2 seconds
Our vision model identifies the material, sub-type, and condition — with a confidence score and clear reasoning.

Get local disposal rules
See which bin in your city, collection days, prep instructions, and nearby drop-off centres.
